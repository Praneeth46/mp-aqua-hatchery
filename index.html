About Our Hatchery

MP Aqua Hatchery is a CAA approved shrimp hatchery located in Vemavaram, Tuni Coast, Andhra Pradesh. We specialize in producing strong and high quality L. Vannamei post larvae using strict hatchery biosecurity and advanced larval rearing techniques.
